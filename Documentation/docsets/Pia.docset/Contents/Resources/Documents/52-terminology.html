Terminology

  • PiA Nets - The SDK. From your reference, a compiled iOS Cocoa Touch Framework.

  • Netaxept - Netaxept is a comprehensive online payment service provided by Nets. It enables merchants to offer their customers different ways to pay purchases easily and securely in webshops, mobile environment and call centre services. When using Netaxept, it is easy to pay with broad range of payment methods and currencies, and still meet high security requirements set for online paying.

  • Easy Payment - After the initial transaction, the customer can initiate the following transaction by logging in to your webshop or mobile app and paying simply with one click or by entering their card’s CVV/CVC security code. Easy Payment is applicable especially for mobile applications where it eases the customer’s purchase experience significantly..

  • Easy (one-click) payment without Security Code (CVV/CVC) - After the initial transaction, the customer can initiate the following transaction by logging in to your webshop or mobile app and paying simply with one click without entering their card’s CVV/CVC security code. Easy (one-click) payment is applicable especially for mobile applications where it eases the customer’s purchase experience significantly..

  • Recurring Payments - After the initial transaction, you as a merchant can charge the customer’s preferred card at specific times based on the agreement you have made with the customer. Recurring Payments is applicable especially for paying subscriptions, membership fees and other regular payments, without the need of customer interaction after the initial payment.

  • Register new card - Users can register or save their cards in advance
